GOALS

OUR CLUB GOALS TO BE OUTCOME

  • To Empower students in achieving effective knowledge about environment.
  • Networking & Communication skills improvement.
  • Motivates and improves the confidence & the self - esteem of students.
  • To educate the students about their immediate environment.
  • To impart knowledge on concepts of eco-system.
  • To involve young learners in effort to preserve environment
  • To create a clean and green consciousness among students.

OBJECTIVES

  • To make a clean and green environment and to involve students in environment related activites.
  • To promote awareness among people about ecology.
  • To innovate ideas among people about ecology and its importance.
  • To motivate the students to keep their surroundings green and clean by undertaking plantation of trees.
  • To maintain cleanliness in and around the campus.
  • To educate students to create awareness among public on saving nature.

BENEFITS

  • We connect you along with the nature.
  • We help your service minded actions to reach to the environment.
  • The impact we create would be higher as well as it also satisfies our individual desire to make environment clean and green.
  • It motivates students to take effort in preserving environment.
  • It helps to improve confidence and communication skills.
